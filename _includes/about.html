libtrancevibe is an open source, cross platform driver for the Rez Trancevibrator and compatible devices. The driver allows the developer to control the speed of the Trance Vibrator in a platform independant way.

Note: Outside of finding it on eBay, the original TranceVibrator is no longer available. We recommend the Drmn Trance Vibrator, which is protocol compatible with libtrancevibe.
